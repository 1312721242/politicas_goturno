Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17-Feb-2026

1. Information We Collect

At GoTurno, we collect only the information necessary to operate the platform:

  • Account and contact data: name, email address, phone number, city and/or reference address (when the user provides it), and profile photo (if the user chooses to upload it).
  • In-app activity: bookings, cancellations, favorites, and actions necessary for the service to function.
  • Location: latitude and longitude only if the user grants permission, to show nearby businesses and improve the experience.
  • Technical identifiers: minimal device technical data needed for security and for notifications to work (for example, push notification tokens).
  • Diagnostics: error logs and technical events for support and service improvement.

2. Use of Information

  • Manage user accounts.
  • Enable business discovery features, bookings, and business administration.
  • Process business owner subscriptions through external providers (for example, Paddle).
  • Improve user experience, security, and fraud prevention.
  • Provide technical support.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

GoTurno does not process or manage payments between users and businesses. GoTurno is not a marketplace and does not act as a financial intermediary.

The only payments associated with the platform correspond to the business owner subscription to access the software tools (SaaS model). Where applicable, charges are processed by external providers (for example, Paddle).

GoTurno does not collect or store sensitive financial information such as full card numbers, security codes, or complete banking details.

Important: If payment by bank transfer (Ecuador) is enabled, GoTurno may request minimal information to verify the payment (for example: receipt/proof of payment, reference, date, and amount), solely to activate the subscription and prevent fraud.

4. Security

  • SSL/HTTPS encryption in communications.
  • Secure authentication via tokens.
  • Reasonable security measures and infrastructure monitoring.

5. Data Sharing

We only share data with essential providers required to operate GoTurno:

  • Paddle (business owner subscription management).
  • Hosting and infrastructure services (operation and storage).
  • Notification services (push notification delivery).

GoTurno does not sell personal data nor share it with data brokers.
